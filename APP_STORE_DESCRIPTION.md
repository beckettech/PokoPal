## Description (4,000 char max)

The ultimate companion for Pokémon Pokopia on Nintendo Switch.

AI-POWERED CHAT
Got a question about Pokopia? Just ask. Our built-in AI chat knows every Pokémon, every habitat, every mechanic — and gives you instant answers. No searching, no digging through wikis. It's like having a Pokopia expert in your pocket.

COMPLETE POKÉDEX
All 300 Pokémon at your fingertips. Browse by name, filter by rarity (Common, Rare, Legendary) or specialty, and search for any creature instantly. Mark the ones you've befriended and watch your completion climb.

212 HABITATS
Every habitat in Pokopia with detailed descriptions. Filter by location — Withered Wastelands, Bleak Beach, Rocky Ridges, Sparkling Skylands, Palette Town, or Cloud Island. See which Pokémon each habitat attracts and track what you've discovered.

INTERACTIVE MAP
Explore the full world of Pokopia. Tap any location to see its habitats and the Pokémon that call it home. Plan your next adventure without switching to the game.

CLOUD ISLANDS
Browse a live feed of island creations from the Pokopia community. See what other trainers are building, get inspired, and share your own dream islands with the world.

ITEMS & MYSTERY GIFTS
Track every item and redeemable Mystery Gift so nothing slips through the cracks.

DREAM ISLANDS
Keep tabs on your Dream Island progress and plan your next build.

FEATURES AT A GLANCE
• AI chat — ask anything about Pokopia
• Full 300-Pokémon Pokédex with smart filters
• 212 habitats with location-based filtering
• Interactive world map
• Cloud Islands community feed
• Items & Mystery Gift tracking
• Dream Islands support
• Dark mode
• Works offline — all data bundled in the app

Pokopia Guide is updated regularly as new content drops.
